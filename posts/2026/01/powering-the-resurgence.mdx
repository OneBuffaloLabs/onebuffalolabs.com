One Buffalo Labs was founded on a simple idea: **Buffalo businesses deserve world-class technology.**

We believe that local institutions shouldn't have to settle for outdated websites or clunky software. This month, we put that belief into practice by launching two major redesigns for staples of the Buffalo community.

## Real Results for Real Businesses

Whether we are optimizing workflows or building high-speed React sites for healthcare providers, our goal is always the same: **Results.**

We don't just "build websites." We build digital foundations.

### 1. Town & Village Pediatrics

We created a digital "Medical Home" that parents can rely on. By moving to a modern Next.js stack, we ensured that dosage charts, urgent care info, and physician availability are accessible instantly—even at 2:00 a.m.

### 2. McClelland Small Animal Hospital

For Buffalo's oldest animal hospital (Est. 1880), the challenge was bridging history with modernity. We delivered a site that honors their 145-year legacy while providing the speed and mobile-first features today's pet owners expect.

From veterinary legacy to pediatric care, we are proud to be the tech partner for the businesses that keep Buffalo moving forward.
