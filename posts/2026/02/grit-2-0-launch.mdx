## Deep Customization (The "2.0" Update)

We didn't just add new modes; we gave you the keys to the car. In the past, the app forced you to track everything. Now, you can toggle features on or off to match your goals.

- **Don't want to step on the scale?** Turn off Weight Tracking.

- **Need to quit a bad habit?** Use the new "Name Your Vice" feature to specifically track what you are cutting out (like soda, sugar, or social media).
